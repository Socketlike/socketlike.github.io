# what are you doing here?

...suddenly, there was nothing.

you start to fall down.

you tried to scream, but nothing came out.

you tried to cry, but nothing came out.

seems like there is nothing else you can do...

<hr>

...it's been a while now.

you're still falling.

you flail your hands around, trying to feel something, anything...

but there is nothing.

<hr>

...it really has been a while, huh.

you're still falling.

usually people in these kinds of situations would have went crazy a while back,

but for some reason, you're strangely calm.

<hr>

...you finally realized.

it's not that you're calm,

it's that, *you feel nothing*.

at some point, the nothingness started to contaminate your soul.

a part of you wishes to stop it from contaminating you further.

but you don't feel like it.

<hr>

...it's been a while again.

you're still falling.

<hr>

...you're still falling.

<hr>

...you're still falling.

you're getting a little bit sleepy.

perhaps it was because you went to sleep late last night.

you feel like it's not so bad to go to sleep.

you drift off to sleep...

<hr>

...and that was the end, of you.

because the moment you decided to rest,

your fate was sealed.

your individual consciousness was the only factor distinguishing you from the nothingness around you,

as both your body and soul had became nothing.
